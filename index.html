About me

I’m David Billington, a qualified counsellor. I offer face-to-face sessions in Horndean, Hampshire, as well as online therapy.

My approach is warm, collaborative and grounded. Together, we explore your thoughts and experiences at a pace that feels safe.

I am a registered member of the British Association for Counselling and Psychotherapy (BACP) and work within their ethical framework.

BACP registered member Adults 18+ Confidential & ethical practice Flexible day & evening appointments

How I work

Therapy is not about being “fixed”. It is about understanding yourself more deeply.

What we do together

Through conversation, reflection and gentle challenge, we explore patterns, emotions and experiences that may be shaping your current difficulties.

Session structure

Sessions are 50 minutes and usually take place weekly at a consistent time. We regularly review our work to ensure it feels helpful and supportive.

Your first session

Before you decide to proceed, I offer a complimentary 20-minute phone call so we can talk through what you’re looking for and any questions you may have.

Reviewing progress

We’ll routinely assess how things are going so you feel supported and clear about the direction of the work.
