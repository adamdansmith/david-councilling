Getting started

Selecting a counsellor can feel overwhelming. Before you decide to proceed, I offer a complimentary 20-minute phone call so we can talk through what you’re looking for and any questions you may have.

Step 1 Free 20-minute call

A chance to talk through what you’re looking for, ask questions, and see whether it feels like a good fit.

Step 2 Your first session

We’ll begin gently, and take time to understand what brings you to counselling and what you would like to change.

Step 3 Ongoing work

Sessions are usually weekly at a consistent time, with regular check-ins to make sure the work is helpful and supportive.

How I work

Everyone experiences challenges and sometimes we need additional support to navigate and overcome them. As a trained psychotherapist, I offer a professional and skilled approach to addressing these challenges through a process of self-exploration and discovery within the therapeutic relationship.

My goal is to help you explore and understand your thoughts, feelings, and behaviours in order to promote healing and growth.

Session length

Sessions last 50 minutes.

Reviewing progress

We’ll routinely assess how things are going, so you feel supported and clear about the direction of our work.
